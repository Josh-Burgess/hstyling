Closet Cleanse & Style

How It Works

During our first session we'll get your closet in shape by sorting the good from the bad and the ugly! We'll reorganize everything so you can easily find what you want when you want it. This session is all about lifting the weight of excess, unnecessary items, and giving you a beautifully organized closet for maximum ease of use.
In session two, we’ll give you some clarity! Once we pull out items that you aren’t sure how to style but want to love or already love, I’ll create a personalized style guide. This guide will include looks with your items, body shape, color analysis, and your style type. We’ll follow this up with a virtual meeting to discuss your personal style guide which includes your style type, body shape analysis, color analysis and the mini lookbook using the items you provided.
 
 

VIP Styling

Personal Styling on Demand

Annual

Imagine having someone on call every time you need advice on what to wear. With this option that is exactly what you’ll have! I’ll be there to support you and your image goals for one full calendar year. We’ll kick this off with a one hour session to discuss your personal style and image goals
This includes
  • two closet cleanses to set you up every six months for a successful season of style choices. We’ll organize and purge for optimal ease of use.
  • dedicated time for me to shop virtually for you each month so your wardrobe stays current and up to date
  • four hours each month of one-to-one planning for all your style needs where we can discuss any challenges and events you’ll need to look your best for
  • a seasonal trend update and personalized look book that translates trends to looks that work best for your preferences and style
Best of all you have unlimited texting and e-mail support. Have a major style emergency? Text me! Out shopping and have a question? Text me! I’ll be there to answer your questions and always make sure you look your very best!

Quarterly

Want to test drive the VIP life? Sign on as a Quarterly VIP! You’ll get many of the same benefits as an Annual VIP but for a period of three months. We’ll start with the one hour session discussing your personal style and image goals. Next we’ll cleanse and set your closet for ease of use.
This also includes
  • dedicated time for me to shop virtually for you each month so your wardrobe stays current and up to date
  • four hours each month of one-to-one planning for all your style needs where we can discuss any challenges or events you’ll need to look your best for
You’ll still have on call to answer any questions via text or e-mail. Unlimited support to always have your style conundrums solved!

About Me

A life in styling

Hi! Hola! Bonjour! My name is Yolandie and I have been styling for what feels like my whole life! As much as we all want others to get to know us and who we are as people, judgments based on our appearance are a fact of reality.
I learned this very young and developed it into a love of all things fashion and a need for constant improvement. This began with a Bachelors degree in Fashion Marketing and has been supplemented by 15 years in retail working with customers and creating inspiring displays. You know that imeppecably dressed mannequin you walk by in a store and think to yourself, I wish I could dress that well, that was me! In addition to all the people I’ve dressed as part of my career in retail, I have also worked on live theatre productions costuming the actors. Lastly, very early in my career I had a brief stint as a dance costume designer.
What does all this experience mean for you? It translates into an extensive expertise in fabric knowledge and fit as well as how that all works on your body and projects the image you desire. Whether that image is professional and powerful for a big meeting or sophisticated and elegant on your wedding day, I am in business to empower you though style!
As Marc Jacobs once said, “clothes mean nothing until someone lives in them.” What do you want your style to say you're living for?
